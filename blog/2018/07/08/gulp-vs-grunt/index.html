Differences between Grunt and Gulp

July 8, 2018 Â· 2 min read
  • The way you configure your tasks. Grunt is configuration-based. Gulp is stream-based.
  • The way they run your tasks. Grunt runs the processes you want to execute in sequential manner. Gulp tries to run them with a maximum concurrency, meaning it will try to execute processes in parallel if possible.

Using stream, Gulp saves a lot of time on disk I/O.
